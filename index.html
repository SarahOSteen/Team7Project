The Sail Loft

About Us

Opened by Sarah O'Steen and Shirley Clinton in 1975, the Sail Loft is where it all began. Boasting an international reputation for serving the freshest fish and shellfish, Head Chef MK Pascoe and our team celebrate the very best Scottish produce, with ingredients sourced locally to create timeless seafood dishes with simple flavours.
